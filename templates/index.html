Nutrition Tips

Hydration

Drink at least 2-3 liters of water daily. Proper hydration supports metabolism and helps control appetite.

Meal Timing

Try to eat every 3-4 hours to maintain energy levels and prevent overeating at main meals.

Portion Control

Use smaller plates and bowls to help control portion sizes without feeling deprived.

Food Variety

Rotate different foods weekly to ensure a wide range of nutrients and prevent dietary boredom.
